How QR Code works?

How could you use a QR code?

You could use QR codes in your business in a number of ways. Do it on every product on your web site containing product details, contacts and URL to the page so they can show their friends on their Smartphones. Add them to your business card or create vCard containing your contact details so its easy for someone to add you on their Smartphone.

Increased engagement with fans by adding them to any print advertising, flyers, posters, invites, TV ads etc containing

  • Product details
  • Contact details
  • Offer details
  • Event details
  • Competition details
  • Twitter, Facebook, Pinterest
  • A link to your YouTube video

QR Codes can be use in whatever you want.
