QR Code Encoding Description

What is QR Code?

A QR code is a specific matrix barcode, readable by dedicated QR barcode readers and Smartphones. The code consists of black modules arranged in a square pattern on a white background. The information encoded can be URL, text, vCard and other data types. QR Code will take your visitors, customers or friends to whatever information or website.
